instructions:
  - "Pat the chicken completely dry with paper towels."
  - "Season chicken evenly with salt."
  - "Lightly coat chicken in flour, shaking off excess."
  - "Heat oil to 175°C (350°F) in a skillet."
  - "Fry chicken until golden and crisp, about 6–8 minutes per side."
  - "Rest chicken on paper towels before serving."

tips:
  - "Dry surfaces brown faster and crisp better."
  - "Rice flour produces a lighter, crunchier crust."
  - "Avoid overcrowding the pan to maintain oil temperature."
