instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C)."
  - "Toss carrots with olive oil, salt, pepper, and cumin."
  - "Spread carrots evenly on a baking sheet."
  - "Roast until tender and caramelized, flipping once."
  - "Drizzle with honey and return to oven briefly."
  - "Finish with apple cider vinegar and chopped parsley."
  - "Rest briefly before serving."

tips:
  - "Avoid crowding the pan to encourage browning."
  - "Add the honey near the end to prevent burning."
